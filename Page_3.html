Additional Information


Confirmation will be received at time of booking, unless booked within 12hrs on the day of travel. In this case confirmation will be received within 24 hours.

Tours are not suitable for people in wheelchairs or with walking difficulties as passengers undertaking boat trips are required to step down onto the boat when boarding.

Occasionally the Basilica will be closed to the public (without notice) because of festivities or high water

It is possible that the walking tour and boat tour will take place separately (over a couple of days).

A dress code is required to enter places of worship and selected museums. No shorts, or sleeveless tops or dresses are allowed, shoulders must be covered for both men and women.You may risk being refused entry if you fail dress appropriately.
